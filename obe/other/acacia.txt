====== acacia ======

===== Definition: =====

A common tree growing in Palestine.  It is the primary source of the durable wood used to construct the Ark of the Covenant.

  * The hard orange-brown wood is ideally suited for cabinetmaking.
  * The KJV uses the name Shittah tree rather than Acacia.

(See also: other)
