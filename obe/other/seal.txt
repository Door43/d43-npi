====== seal, to seal ======

===== Definition: =====

A seal is an object or substance that is used to close a letter or other object to keep it from being opened.that represents a king or other authoritya symbol of a particular entity; an individual or an organization can have its own seal as a form of representation.

  * Often a seal is marked with a design in some way to show who it belongs to.
  * To seal something means to close that object and keep it closed with something that makes it impossible to open without breaking the seal.
  * Wax that was melted and then allowed to harden was often used to seal letters or other documents that needed to be protected.
  * A seal can also be a special mark that is used to identify someone as belonging to someone else.
  * Paul refers to the Holy Spirit as a seal that shows that our salvation is secure.
