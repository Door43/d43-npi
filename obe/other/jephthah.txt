====== Jephthah ======

===== Facts: =====

Jephthah, a warrior from Gilead who served as a "judge," delivered the Israelites from the Ammonites and defeated the Ephraimites

  * He is celebrated in Hebrews 11:32 as one of the chief deliverer's of his people.
  * Jepthah sacrificed his only child, a daughter, in fulfillment of a vow he had made to God.
